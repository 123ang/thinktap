### Colors (Fire Theme)
- **Primary Red**: `#DC2626` (Red-600) - Main CTAs, primary actions, fire core
- **Gold**: `#F59E0B` (Amber-500) - Premium features, highlights, Pro plan badge
- **Yellow**: `#FCD34D` (Amber-300) - Accents, secondary CTAs, energy
- **Deep Red**: `#991B1B` (Red-800) - Darker accents, hover states
- **Light Gold**: `#FDE68A` (Amber-200) - Backgrounds, subtle highlights
- **Fire Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #DC2626 0%, #F59E0B 50%, #FCD34D 100%)` - Hero backgrounds, CTAs
- **Neutral Dark**: `#1F2937` - Text, headings
- **Neutral Light**: `#F9FAFB` - Card backgrounds, sections
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Card backgrounds, text on colored backgrounds

### Fire Element Inspiration
- **Logo**: Flame or spark icon in red/gold gradient
- **Icons**: Fire/flame motifs for energy and engagement
- **Animations**: Flickering effects, flame-like gradients
- **Visuals**: Warm, energetic, passionate feeling

### Fire-Themed Visual Elements

**Logo Design**:
- Primary: Stylized flame or spark icon
- Colors: Fire gradient (red #DC2626 â†’ gold #F59E0B â†’ yellow #FCD34D)
- Animation: Subtle flicker/pulse effect

**Background Patterns**:
- Subtle fire gradient overlays
- Spark particle effects (optional, subtle)
- Warm gradient backgrounds (red â†’ gold â†’ yellow)
- Fire texture overlays at low opacity

**Button Styles**:
- Primary: Gold (#F59E0B) with fire gradient on hover
- Secondary: Red outline (#DC2626) with gold fill on hover
- Premium: Full fire gradient (red â†’ gold â†’ yellow)
- Hover: Flame glow effect, slight scale

**Icon Treatment**:
- Fire/flame icons for key features
- Gold/yellow accents on standard icons
- Spark effects on hover

**Card Accents**:
- Thin fire gradient borders
- Gold corner accents
- Subtle fire glow on hover
- Warm shadow colors (red/gold tinted)

### Typography
- **Headings**: `Inter` or `Poppins` - Bold, 700 weight
- **Body**: `Inter` - Regular, 400 weight
- **CTA Buttons**: `Inter` - Semibold, 600 weight

### Spacing
- Container max-width: `1280px`
- Section padding: `80px` vertical (mobile: `40px`)
- Element spacing: `24px` between major elements

### FAQ Content

**Q: Do students need to create an account?**  
A: No! Students can join sessions instantly using a 6-digit code. Only lecturers need an account to create and manage sessions.

**Q: What happens to my sessions on the free plan?**  
A: On the free plan, you can run unlimited live sessions, but session history is not saved after the session ends. Upgrade to ThinkTap Pro to access full history and analytics.

**Q: Can I use ThinkTap on mobile?**  
A: Yes! We have dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, plus a fully responsive web app that works on any device.

**Q: How many students can join a session?**  
A: All plans support unlimited students per session. There's no cap on participation.

**Q: Can I export results?**  
A: Yes, on ThinkTap Pro and higher plans, you can export session results and analytics as PDF or CSV files.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards, FPX online banking, and bank transfers for Faculty and University plans.

**Q: Is there a money-back guarantee?**  
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**  
A: Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.
